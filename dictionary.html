Slang Dictionary

OMG

Oh My God — expresses surprise, shock, or excitement.

Rizz

Ability to attract or charm someone.

Cap

A lie or something untrue; “no cap” means you're telling the truth.

IDK

I Don't Know — used when you're unsure about something.

WTF

What The F*** — expresses confusion or disbelief.

LMAO

Laughing My Ass Off — used when something is extremely funny.

TTYL

Talk To You Later — used when ending a conversation.

SMH

Shaking My Head — used when something is disappointing or silly.

Sigma

A person who operates independently and confidently.

Bet

Means “alright,” “okay,” or “sounds good.”

ROFL

Rolling On the Floor Laughing — stronger version of LMAO.

No Cap

Used to say you're being honest.

FOMO

Fear Of Missing Out — feeling worried you're missing something fun or important.

On God

Used to strongly express truthfulness.

BRB

Be Right Back — used when you're stepping away briefly.

GG

Good Game — said after a match; can also be sarcastic.

Noob

A beginner or someone inexperienced, usually in gaming.

AFK

Away From Keyboard — not currently present or active.

ICYMI

In Case You Missed It — used when sharing info someone might've missed.

Bet

Used to show agreement or challenge; like “alright” or “watch me.”

W

Short for “win”; used when something is good or a victory.

L

Short for “loss”; used when something is bad or embarrassing.

Sus

Suspicious or sketchy — popularized by Among Us.

Yeet

To throw something with force; also an expression of hype.

Bae

A term of endearment for someone special; “before anyone else.”

Salty

Used when someone is bitter, upset, or annoyed.

Ghost

To disappear from a convo or relationship without saying anything.

Flex

To show off, usually in a braggy way.

Lowkey

Something subtle or not openly expressed; opposite of highkey.

Highkey

Something obvious, strong, or openly expressed.

Vibe

The feeling, mood, or atmosphere of something.

Extra

Over-the-top behavior; too dramatic or excessive.

Thirsty

Acting desperate for attention or affection.

Snack

A very attractive person; looks good enough to “eat.”
